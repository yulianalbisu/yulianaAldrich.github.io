UI / UX Design

Design thinking to build the perfect product

At the foundation of effective digital design lies a nuanced understanding of both UI (User Interface) and UX (User Experience).

UI Design

UI design focuses on the visual elements—the look and feel—of a product, be it a screen or webpage. This entails expertise in areas such as font sizes, color themes, font hierarchy, image placement, and the components. The goal? To create a visually appealing interface that resonates with your brand, is user-friendly, and intuitively calls users to engage. A well-crafted UI ensures consistency across devices, reinforcing your product's brand identity and enhancing the overall user experience. The tools needed: Figma or Zeplin to develop a LoFi-HiFi wireframe, where you can see the layout and make sure goes with the brand of your product. Miro and sticky notes (when in person) to catch all the ideas and thoughts for later represent it as a prototype.
One thing I always check is the accessibility guideliness, we need to make technology accessible for all!

Design Thinking, after I performed user research, I backup they project with a storm of creative ideas to feel the user's applying empathy maps, customer journey and color theory. Based on the brand theme, typographies and layout. And then...

Lo-Fi design, first sketch creationg using a sketchpad (my favorite, allows me to think and innovate better), Figma or Miro for me, being a UI designer is a dynamic blend of fun and collaboration. I enjoy scrambling ideas and working closely with the team to shape visually compelling designs, turning each project into an exciting adventure

In the realm of UI design, "hifi design" stands for high-fidelity design — a serious and detailed approach aimed at communicating the true essence of an application. It involves meticulously addressing usability, envisioning how the design sequence unfolds across various devices. Constant communication with stakeholders and team members is key, adapting the design based on valuable feedback. Emphasizing a team effort, hifi design acknowledges the importance of iteration, where taking feedback and refining designs is a crucial part of the creative process.

UX Design

The User Experience covers how the User will interact with your product. As a UX Designer, focus on the flow in the feel of the application, Does the navigation feels logical? Is the overall user experience simple and aesthetic?
